DSL-Comet
Domain-specific modelling in mobile devices








Information


DSL-comet is an iOS tool that allows graphical, collaborative modelling on mobile devices.
Our architecture permits creating both Sirius and mobile editors from a single description.
Models can be stored locally or in a server, and they are compatible with EMF
